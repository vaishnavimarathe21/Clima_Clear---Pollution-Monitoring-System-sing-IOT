Breathe Beyond Pollution

At the forefront of innovation, our Pollution Monitoring System redefines air quality monitoring. We specialize in detecting harmful pollutants in real time with precision and reliability. Our team works tirelessly to provide accurate data that empowers communities to act. From urban cities to rural areas, we aim to make clean air accessible to all. With cutting-edge technology and a commitment to sustainability, we bridge awareness and action. Join us in creating healthier environments for everyone, everywhere. Together, let’s breathe cleaner, fresher air and shape a better tomorrow!



Why you use our System?

1. Real-Time Monitoring Your system provides real-time updates on air quality, empowering users with accurate and instant data about harmful pollutants in their environment.

2. IoT-Driven Efficiency With the power of IoT, your system offers seamless connectivity, enabling users to access air quality data remotely through apps or web platforms anytime, anywhere.

3. Health Protection By identifying pollution levels early, your system helps individuals and communities take proactive steps to reduce exposure to harmful pollutants, safeguarding their health.

4. Community and Environmental Awareness Your system promotes environmental consciousness by giving users the knowledge they need to advocate for cleaner air and more sustainable practices.

5. Cost-Effective and Scalable IoT integration ensures that the system is not only affordable to deploy but also scalable for use in homes, businesses, and public spaces.



Working of Our System

In your Air Pollution Monitoring System using IoT, different gas sensors (such as MQ series or CCS811) are used to detect pollutants like CO, NO₂, SO₂, O₃, and PM2.5. The sensors are connected to an ESP8266 Wi-Fi module, which collects the data and sends it over the internet to a cloud server. The cloud platform processes the data and displays real-time air quality information on a web interface. Users can monitor current gas levels, view historical trends, and receive alerts when pollutant levels exceed safe limits. This system ensures continuous air quality monitoring and raises awareness for a cleaner environment.
